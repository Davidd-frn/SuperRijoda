Project Description

By Fournier David, Dozinel Jonathan, Murtezani Rinor

Super Rijoda is a 2D platformer inspired by classic games like Super Mario Bros and Rayman. The player controls Rijoda, a small hero who must travel through challenging levels filled with enemies, obstacles, and hidden power-ups to save his world.

The game focuses on a simple and accessible gameplay style, enhanced by our own unique mechanics and retro visuals.

The main features of the game include:

  • Character Control: The player moves through the level using classic platforming controls — jumping, running, and collecting transformation power-ups that grant temporary abilities.
  • Level Progression: The game includes a series of levels with increasing difficulty, new environments, and enemies. Each level is handcrafted to provide unique challenges.
  • Enemies: Enemies appear throughout the game and try to block the player’s path. Some chase the player, while others present platforming challenges.
  • Health System: The health system is inspired by Mario. The player starts with a basic transformation. If hit while transformed, they revert to the basic form. If hit in the basic form, they lose a life. This makes collecting and keeping transformations a key part of survival.

All these features come together to offer a fun, nostalgic, and engaging experience that blends the best of old-school platformers with a modern twist.

Super Rijoda is developed as part of the Web Development course at HES-SO Valais, and it pays tribute to the golden era of pixel-art games while introducing new ideas and gameplay variety.

You can find the project's source code and follow our progress on GitHub.
